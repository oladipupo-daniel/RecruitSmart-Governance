The 10 EDPB Screening Criteria
Evaluation or Scoring: Are you assessing people based on characteristics, performance, or behavior?

RecruitSmart: YES (The Match-Score).

Automated Decision-Making with Legal/Significant Effect: Does the AI make a decision that could get someone a job or lose them an opportunity?

RecruitSmart: YES (Filtering candidates).

Systematic Monitoring: Is the processing used to observe, monitor, or control data subjects? (e.g., tracking a candidate's clicks on the portal).

Sensitive Data / Highly Personal Data: Does it involve political opinions, health data, or private communications?

Data Processed on a Large Scale: Does it involve a high number of individuals, a large volume of data, or a long duration?

RecruitSmart (for MDAs): YES (National-level recruitment).

Matching or Combining Datasets: Are you linking data from different sources (e.g., pulling a candidate's LinkedIn data and matching it with their CV)?

Data Concerning Vulnerable Data Subjects: Does it involve children, employees (power imbalance), or asylum seekers?

RecruitSmart: YES (The employer-employee relationship is considered a "vulnerable" power imbalance by the CNIL).

Innovative Use or Applying New Technological/Organizational Solutions: Does it use AI, Biometrics, or IoT in a way that hasn't been standard before?

RecruitSmart: YES (NLP and Deep Learning).

International Transfers Outside the EU/EEA: Does data move to countries without an "Adequacy Decision"?

Prevents Data Subjects from Exercising a Right or Using a Service: Does the processing decide if someone can enter a contract or access a service?

RecruitSmart: YES (Access to an employment contract).
